Visit Modalities

To make the most of your CERN visit, please be aware of the following:

  • Booking: All visits to CERN are free of charge. However, booking is mandatory for all guided tours and must be made in advance through the official CERN website.
  • Activities: CERN offers a wide range of activities, including permanent and temporary exhibitions, science shows, and lab workshops. Some activities may require on-the-spot booking, so be sure to check the daily schedule upon arrival.
  • Planning: For detailed information on opening hours, how to get to CERN, and accessibility, please refer to the official CERN website.

Activities and Guided Tours

CERN offers a variety of tours and activities. Here’s a summary of what you can expect:

  • Synchrocyclotron: Discover CERN’s first accelerator, the Synchrocyclotron, and learn about the early history of the laboratory.
  • ATLAS Control Centre: Get a behind-the-scenes look at the control centre for the ATLAS experiment, one of the detectors that discovered the Higgs boson.
  • Booking Tours: Guided tours are free but in high demand. You can register for a tour on a first-come, first-served basis upon arrival at the CERN Science Gateway via a dedicated web app. It is recommended to arrive early to increase your chances.
  • Underground Visits: Please note that underground visits are not available for individual visitors.
  • Other Activities: For more information on other activities, such as science shows and lab workshops, please check the official CERN website.

Important Notes

  • Book train tickets and CERN tours well in advance.
  • Accommodation near CERN is limited, so book early.
  • Verify the CERN shuttle schedule.
